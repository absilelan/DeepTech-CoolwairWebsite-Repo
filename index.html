About Coolwair // 001

Pioneering Mobile
Thermal Intelligence

Coolwair is an early-stage deeptech startup developing battery-efficient, hybrid-powered thermoregulation for mobile environments. Our proprietary dual-mode architecture combines Alternate Current (AC) and Direct Current (DC) in a compact energy management system — enabling sustained cooling where it was never possible before.

We've built a modular thermal management stack that integrates hardware, power electronics, and embedded control systems — adaptable across personal, industrial, defense, and healthcare applications.
